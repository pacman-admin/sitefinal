For MacOS users

Pac-Man is blocked by MacOS Gatekeeper. You must either disable Gatekeeper with `sudo spctl --master-disable` or go to System Preferences, click "Security & Privacy", click General, and click "Open Anyway". Both of these options require an admin password.

WARNING: Disabling gatekeeper will leave your Mac vulnerable to potential viruses. PROCEED AT YOUR OWN RISK
